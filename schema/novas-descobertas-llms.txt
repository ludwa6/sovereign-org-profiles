# Novas Descobertas Association

> Novas Descobertas Association is a Portuguese educational NGO founded in 1994, based at Campo do Vale, Quinta Vale da Lama, Odiáxere, Lagos, SW Algarve, Portugal. It delivers experiential education and self-development programs for learners of all ages and backgrounds, including youth camps, farm school days, a Permaculture Design Course (PDC), Training in Ecosystem Regeneration, Facilitation and Leadership Training, and Erasmus+ exchange programs. Programs are offered in English and Portuguese.

## Programs Offered

- Permaculture Design Course (PDC) — internationally recognised certification
- Training in Ecosystem Regeneration — field-based restoration practice
- Facilitation and Leadership Training — group process and community leadership
- Residential summer camps for youth
- Summer day camps for youth
- Days at the Farm — school and group programs
- Easter day camps
- Internships
- Erasmus+ exchange programs (Vias Novas Descobertas, Leadership in Regenerative Learning Communities, RegenerAction)
