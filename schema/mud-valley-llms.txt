# Mud Valley Foundation

> Mud Valley Foundation is a nonprofit based at Quinta Vale da Lama, Odiáxere, Lagos, SW Algarve, Portugal, focused on education and action-oriented initiatives for ecosystem restoration in southwestern Portugal. It runs the Ecosystem Regeneration Mondays volunteer program, the Ecosystem Regeneration Camp, the Barlavento Ecosystem Regeneration Community, and the Bioregional Learning Center for SW Portugal. It also works on building community resilience to wildfires.

## What Mud Valley Foundation Does

- Ecosystem Regeneration Mondays — weekly volunteer sessions at Quinta Vale da Lama
- Ecosystem Regeneration Camps — immersive field programs in restoration practice
- Barlavento Ecosystem Regeneration Community — regional community of practice
- Bioregional Learning Center — knowledge hub for SW Portugal ecosystem restoration
- Wildfire resilience programs — landscape-scale prevention and community preparedness
- Desertification reversal through regenerative land management
