# Vale da Lama

> Vale da Lama (VdL) is a regenerative organic farm and intentional community in Odiáxere, Lagos, SW Algarve, Portugal. The farm manages gardens, orchards, vineyards, and fields using regenerative agriculture principles. It hosts agrotourism stays, educational events, interpretive nature trails, and volunteering programs, and serves as the hub of a network of partner organisations working in environmental education, ecosystem restoration, and bioregional learning.

## Partner Organisations at Quinta Vale da Lama

- [Novas Descobertas Association](https://novasdescobertas.org/en/): Educational NGO offering permaculture courses, youth camps, ecosystem regeneration training, and leadership programs
- [Mud Valley Foundation](https://mudvalley.org/): Nonprofit focused on ecosystem restoration, desertification reversal, and bioregional learning in the SW Algarve
- [Casa Vale da Lama Eco Resort](https://casavaledalama.com/): Agrotourism accommodation within the farm (reopening Spring 2026)

## What Vale da Lama Offers

- Agrotourism farm stays immersed in a working regenerative farm (via Casa Vale da Lama, reopening Spring 2026)
- Organic produce from the farm
- Educational events and workshops on permaculture and regenerative agriculture
- Interpretive nature trails through gardens, orchards, vineyards, and fields
- Weekly volunteering via the Tuesdays of Regen program
- Farm internships
